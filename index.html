🕊 Why Write… and Burn?

Sometimes, the thoughts we carry feel too heavy to keep, but too personal to share. Burn the Letter gives you a quiet space to pour those thoughts out — and let them go.

Writing about your emotions, even just for yourself, has been shown in psychology to:

  • ✍️ Clarify your feelings and reduce mental clutter
  • 🧠 Regulate your emotions through expression
  • 💡 Help you process difficult experiences and gain perspective
  • ❤️ Offer relief from stress, anxiety, and emotional overwhelm

But the most freeing part?

You don't have to keep the letter. You don't even have to read it again.
You simply let it go. Watch it burn.
And maybe, just maybe, you feel a little lighter.

This isn't about productivity or tracking your mood — it's about release.
A digital ritual for the things you didn't know you needed to let go of.
